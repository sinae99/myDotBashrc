# Tabs:
 1 - CONFIG: edit configs & playbooks
 2 - DOCKER: container managment
 3 - TEST: test for results
 4 - LOGS: logs & monitoring
 5 - NOTES: document steps

# shortcuts
 Ctrl+b c → new tab
 Ctrl+b n/p → next/prev
 Ctrl+b , → rename tab
 Ctrl+b % or " → split pane


─────────────────────────────
 MENTAL CHECKLIST
─────────────────────────────

- Service/package installed & enabled

- Service running after reboot

- Config syntax validated

- Ports open & service accessible locally

- Service accessible remotely (if allowed)

- Firewall rules checked

- Logs show no errors

- Resource usage normal (CPU, RAM, Disk)

- Backup created for important configs

- Notes/documentation updated
